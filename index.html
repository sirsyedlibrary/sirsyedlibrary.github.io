About Our Library

The Central Library of Sir Syed College, Taliparamba, serves as a vibrant hub of academic resources, knowledge sharing, and intellectual growth. With a collection of over 43,000 books, the library supports the learning, teaching, and research needs of students, faculty, research scholars, and staff across all disciplines.

The library provides a calm and welcoming environment for study, along with a range of services including book lending, reservation facilities, previous year question papers, and a dedicated computer lab with 24 systems for academic use. Our users check in and out using a barcode-based e-gate system, ensuring seamless access and usage tracking.

To enhance communication and user engagement, the library has introduced WhatsApp-based services for alerts, reservation updates, and reader interaction. We also maintain an active presence online through our website and social media, sharing updates on new arrivals, reading trends, and library events.

Driven by a commitment to digital transformation and user-focused service, the library continues to adopt innovative tools and technologies, making it a cornerstone of the academic community at Sir Syed College.
